since i started blogging a little over 5 years ago i have used jekyll to render this blog. jekyll is a static blog engine that allows one to write post in markdown. Since i don’t blog that much it was a small yak shave every time i did blog as jekyll changed enough in the times between eáºƒhen i did blog as it was a slight hassle.

A couple of weeks ago I was helping a colleague get a small interal blog set up but the amount of frustration we had getting it up and running was way more than was necessary. I remember reading somewhere about hugo a static site generator written in golang. We decided to give it a spin and was pleasantly suprised at how much easier it is to get started when your blogging platform has all it’s dependencies baked into one executable. Well everything but the themes.

This recomendation was given before i had tried it myself so i decided to spend a couple of hours to migrate my blog under easter weekend from jekyll to hugo. It was pretty smooth i had everything migrated after a couple of hours and an hour more to get the same design on the new and old blog.

I don’t know how long i will keep this or if in a couple of years another blog platform will convince me to migrate again or if i decide to write my own. It really gets down to what i like more yak shaving or writting and some days there’s not much differnce.
