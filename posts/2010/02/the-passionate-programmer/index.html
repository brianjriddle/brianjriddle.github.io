finished the passionate programmer yesterday. It’s been 6 years since i read the pragmatic programmer and from what i remember of it the passionate programmer is a great companion book. This must be one of the first books i have read from some one that has had many different roles in the software industry. Chad Fowler has been a programmer, manager, CTO, headhunter and consultant.

The chapters were quite short and focused with homework/tips/assignments at the end of most of the chapters. Would recommend this book to anybody that is feeling a little down about their job as it’s a little like chicken soup for the soul.
