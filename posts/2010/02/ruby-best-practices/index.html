finished reading Gregory T. Brown’s ruby best practices. After reading programming ruby: the pragmatic programmers guide a couple of years ago and the ruby way in the spring of 2009 it’s definitely a worth while read and is going to be well thumbed threw.

I really liked that the book started off with a whole chapter on unit testing. Chapters 2 and 3 were a little difficult but mainly because i don’t use ruby enough daily these chapters will definitely mean more study :). The rest of the chapters are practical enough to that i will start using them pretty much immediately.
