19 Feb 2010

Just finished reading stephen covey’s 7 habits of highly effective people. I was not prepared for what I had bought. I thought that I bought a book that was going to give me practical tools to be more productive.

Luckily I could not have been more wrong. This book was more about basic internal characteristics that highly effective people have. It was the first self help book I had ever read. I agree the values/principals laid out as I see them at least in others. It’s written with tons of both person and 2rd party stories interwoven with the theories presented.

Reading it after having lived in Europe for 10+ years coupled with some pretty strong agnostic tendencies made me almost put it down and not return but i plowed on.

Next book is Gregory T. Brown’s ‘ruby best practices’.
