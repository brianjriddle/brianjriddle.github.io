advanced tdd with uncle bob day three

today more physics, TDD concepts and tools, adding new functions to hunt the wumpus. The first part of the morning we went over coverage and metrics. Here we looked at emma, crap4j and discussed The Braithwaite Correlation. The second part of the morning we looked at TDD vs BDD, GUI testing, legacy code, and convincing others to use TDD. TDD vs BDD came down to semantics and readability. Sure there are lots of devil in the detail but it comes down to a matter of style.

advanced tdd with unclebob day two

Today more physics and theory. The physics covered the speed of light and Einstein’s special and general relativity. TDD theory was about what makes a well behaved unit test ie F.I.R.S.T, test conventions (where to place them, how to name them). Where to place them unclebob likes to place them right next to the files being tested but most conventions (ie maven) place them in a separate directory. This is kinda a moot point as modern java editors can present a combined view of tests and packages.

The day started with our first lesson in quantum mechanics.
